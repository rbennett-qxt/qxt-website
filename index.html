About Qixent

Qixent (pronounced "Qwix-ent") is a Chicago-based insurtech on a mission to transform how modern insurance is delivered.

We believe insurance should be valuable to customers and deeply embedded in the customer experience.

With our platform, companies of all sizes—from startups to enterprises—can unlock new revenue streams, build trust with customers, and use innovative insurance solutions to deliver more value to their customers.
